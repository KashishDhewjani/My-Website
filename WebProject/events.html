Events

Every term there are a range of regional and local events that are organised by the Computing Hubs. These events are organised for teachers and students alike. They provide attendees with an opportunity to learn special skills, interact with inspiring mentors from the field and unique networking opportunities. So don't miss out and register for one of our events today!
